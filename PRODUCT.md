# Product Overview: Halo Veronica

## What It Is
A minimal personal landing page — two sections, one message. A heartfelt digital gesture built to be shared directly with one person: Veronica.

## Users & Audience
- **Creator**: The person building and sharing the page
- **Recipient**: Veronica — the sole intended audience

## Brand & Tone
- Warm, intimate, and sincere
- Simple and distraction-free
- Romantic — every element serves the message

## Core Content
1. **Hero Section** — Headline: "Halo Veronica"
2. **Section 2** — Declaration: "I Love You"

## Scope & Positioning
A single-page static site. Two sections only. No navigation, no complex interactivity — pure emotional impact through radical simplicity.

## Strategic Principles
- Less is more: two sections, nothing superfluous
- Emotional resonance over feature richness
- Personal, direct, unmistakably made for her
