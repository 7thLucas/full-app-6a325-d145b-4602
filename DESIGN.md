# Design Guidelines: Halo Veronica

## Visual Philosophy
Radical simplicity. Every pixel serves the message. No clutter, no distraction — just warmth and honesty.

## Color Palette
- Background: Deep rich warm dark — near-black with a warm undertone (#0D0A0B or similar)
- Primary text: Soft warm white / cream (#F5EFE6)
- Accent: Rose gold / dusty pink (#C9848A or #D4A0A0)
- Subtle gradient: Warm dark to slightly lighter warm dark for depth

## Typography
- Hero heading ("Halo Veronica"): Large serif or elegant display font — think Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond, or similar. Size: very large (6xl–8xl). Weight: light to regular for elegance.
- Declaration ("I Love You"): Same elegant serif. Size: 5xl–7xl. Centered. Possibly with subtle letter-spacing.
- Overall feel: Editorial, romantic, not overly decorative

## Elevation & Space
- Full-viewport sections (100vh each)
- Generous vertical centering — content sits in the exact middle of each section
- Minimal padding on sides, content centered horizontally
- No borders, no cards, no shadows — flat and intimate

## Components
- Two full-screen sections, stacked vertically
- Section 1: "Halo Veronica" — hero heading, centered, possibly with a subtle subtitle or decorative line underneath
- Section 2: "I Love You" — declaration, large, centered, possibly with a small decorative element (rose, heart, or typographic ornament)
- No header, no footer, no navigation bar
- Smooth scroll behavior between sections

## Animation (optional, subtle)
- Fade-in on load for the hero text
- Gentle fade-in as Section 2 scrolls into view
- Nothing flashy — understated elegance only
